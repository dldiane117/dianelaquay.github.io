Project #1: Cyclistic Bike-Share Project

For this case study, I am a junior data analyst at a fictional bike-share company called Cyclistics. The director of marketing wants to launch a marketing campaign that will convert casual riders (who only rent the bikes for one ride) into annual members. She tells me and the team of other marketing analysts to analyze the given dataset, compare and contrast how casual and annual members use Cyclistic bikes, report our findings, and make suggestions on how Cyclistic can achieve its business goal. In this project I will take you through the analysis process from start to finsh.
